## Issue

## Resolution

- As the first step, please contact your hardware vendor and request a hardware check.

- It is difficult to make a definitive conclusion from this vmcore file alone, but we cannot entirely rule out the possibility that faulty hardware (e.g., CPU, DIMM module, or a firmware bug) may or may not be the underlying cause of the inconsistent state of the lock, leading to the lockup.
