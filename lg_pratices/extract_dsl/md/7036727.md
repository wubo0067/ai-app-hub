## Resolution

- Thehost_edma_drvmodule is not shipped by Red Hat, hence it is recommended to engage the 3rd party moduleprovider(probably Huawei) for further investigation on this issue.

`host_edma_drv`
## Root Cause

- The kernel panic due to hard lockup while trying to acquire the queued spin lock insidepci_bus_write_config_dword()function.
- Here we can seepci_bus_write_config_dword()function was called by theedma_host_dma_transfer()function of 3rd partymodulehost_edma_drv.
- It appears that theedma_host_dma_transfer()has already acquired the lock and not released it before calling thepci_bus_write_config_dword()function causing this issue.
