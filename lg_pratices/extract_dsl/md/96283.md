## Resolution

This issue is being investigated underRed Hat Private Bug 1263522 - netconsole on bnx2 will always panic.


### Workaround

Disable netconsole.


## Root Cause

Thebnx2driver does not contain a protection to stopbnx2_poll()being called when the device and/or driver are not ready to rx/tx traffic.

`bnx2``bnx2_poll()`
## Diagnostic Steps

- NIC firmware hangs for some reason
- bnx2_reset_task()is called to reset hardware from NETDEV WATCHDOG
- Driver prints messages as part of reset
- printk()is called to output those messages
- netconsole uses itsnetpollto callbnx2_polldirectly
- bnx2_pollenters a device init call path and hangs on aspin_lockfor thephy_lock
- Thephy_lockis already held bybnx2_init_nicwhich was earlier in the call chain
