## Resolution

- It is due to race bug which is fixed in the below errataRHSA-2017:1842 - Security Advisory: kernel-3.10.0-693.el7
- Related upstream patchhttps://lore.kernel.org/patchwork/patch/713721/

- RHSA-2017:1842 - Security Advisory: kernel-3.10.0-693.el7

- https://lore.kernel.org/patchwork/patch/713721/
