## Resolution

- The issue appears to be rooted in the hardware. As such, please engage the respective vendor
- It is also advisable to run a hardware diagnostics and check for any underlying problems

- In most investigations, Red Hat Support was unable to explain the actual cause of the kernel panicAs the common denominator across all such cases was theASRockRackhardware, it is needed to reach out to the vendor to confirm if there is an underlying issue, BIOS-related bug, or some other problem
