## Resolution

- The resolution is unknown at this time.
- A possible mitigation measure would be to disable irqbalance.
- It's advisable to contact VMware support to check if the hypervisor experienced excessive high-load conditions around the time frame of the occurrence on the VM..


## Root Cause

- The hard LOCKUP occurred where one CPU was in spinwait of irq_desc structure's ->lock spinlock where another CPU was holding that lock and executing __pci_msix_desc_mask_irq() but there is no possible dead loop or similar scenario where the spinlock remains locked in this code path.
