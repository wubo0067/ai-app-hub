## Environment

- RHEL7.1 (kernel-3.10.0-229.el7)
- TrendMicro ServerProtect for Linux


## Issue

- System hangs where lots of tasks are waiting for page writeback to complete where vsapiapp is blocking in spinlock and prevent kthreadd from taking CPU time to go ahead.


## Resolution

- Engage TrendMicro support team and check with the team for some splxmod bug and errata.
- Red Hat do not have access nor maintain splxmod source.

## Root Cause

- Lots of tasks are waiting for page writeback to comple.
- Those tasks are waiting calling io_schedule() and waiting until another part of the kernel complete what they wait for.
- kworkers will complete what those tasks are waiting for to complete.
- At that time kworkers are calling kthread_create_on_node --> wait_for_completion() and waiting for the completion.
- kthreadd will complete what those kworkers are waiting for to complete.
- At that time vsapiapp calling ioctlMod() from the 3rd party module [splxmod] and calling kvm_lock_spinning() and waiting on spinlock.
- At that time kthreadd is waking up on the CPU where vsapiapp is waiting on spinlock and blocking.
