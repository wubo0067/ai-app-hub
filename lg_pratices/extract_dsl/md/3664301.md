## Resolution

- This problem is recorded in Red Hat internal Bugzilla [1622609].
- The fix is include in kernel-3.10.0-896.el7  RHEL 7.5 (unofficial)
- Upgrade to RHEL7.6(Kernel-3.10.0-957)
- Contact Red Hat technical support to understand more on the problem and progress.


## Root Cause

- The task holding thetvec_baselock in__run_timers()code is looping because the fieldtimer_jiffiesis way behind the current jiffies.
- It goes through the while loop calling the cascade() sequence for each jiffies that it is behind until timer_jiffies is caught up to current jiffies.
