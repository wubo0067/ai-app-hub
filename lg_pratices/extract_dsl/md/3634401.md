## Resolution

- Update to XenServer 7.6.0


#### OR

- WORKAROUND: Usenosmpkernel parameter.

`nosmp`
## Root Cause

- Exact root cause is unknown, but there are chances that older XenServer version does not have support for L1TF CVE, hence updating to latest XenServer resolved the issue.
