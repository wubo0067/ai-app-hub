## Resolution

- Upgrade tokernel-3.10.0-1160.21.1.el7fromRHSA-2021:0856or later.
- No known workaround exists to mitigate the issue until an upgrade can occur.

`kernel-3.10.0-1160.21.1.el7`
## Root Cause

The placement of deferrable timers in a timer vector istechnically incorrect leading to a deadlock.
