## Environment

- Red Hat Enterprise Linux 8
- Dell

## Issue

- The system becomes unresponsive or experiences a kernel panic due to a hard lockup when using DellEMC PowerFlex(scini)?


## Resolution

- Engage the vendor DELL and fix the issue with Powerflex.


## Root Cause

- The cause of the hang/panic state of the system is excessive printing of errors on the console by DellEMC PowerFlex(scini).
- Different types of errors reported but most of the time it was:PowerFlex netSock_GetMyAddress:1776 :Error: Failed to retrieve socket local address for socket 00000000559ecfe0 with Errno 16.
