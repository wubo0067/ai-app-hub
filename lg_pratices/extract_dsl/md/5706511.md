## Resolution

- Engage samfs vendor to understand why it is returning an error (-61, ENODATA)  to nfsd.
- Thebug  1850430related to the WARNING message has been fixed on recently releasedERRATA


## Root Cause

- These warnings appear related to the fact that underlying samfs is returning this error (-61, ENODATA) to nfsd, and this error is not mapped and cannot be understood by NFS.
