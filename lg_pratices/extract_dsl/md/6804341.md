## Resolution

- This issue is due to a slow or over-committed hypervisor.  Please reach out to your VMware hypervisor support team or vendor for resolution.
- For further information on hypervisor overcommitment, please seeVirtualization lags and hypervisor overcommitment.
- As a workaround, increasingwatchdog_thresholdcan be tried.What is the kernel hard/soft lockup detector?

`watchdog_threshold`- What is the kernel hard/soft lockup detector?


## Root Cause

- The system was stalled, very likely due to vCPU starvation from the hypervisor.


## Diagnostic Steps

- Hard lockup is where a CPU is stuck in kernel mode for 10+ seconds. Typically it is looping in kernel mode. Here we see it is spinning busywaiting on a spinlock in VMware pvscsi driver.
