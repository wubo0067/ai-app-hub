## Resolution

- Red Hat does not shipvxodm. Engage module vendor for further investigation and troubleshooting of the issue.

`vxodm`
## Root Cause

- The analysis of vmcore shows that this system crashed due to hard lockup while it was trying to acquire a spinlock in the functionodm_rwsleep_unlock()while it was accessingvxodmfsfilesystem.
- However, theodm_rwsleep_unlock()function is part of third-party Kernel module[vxodm], andvxodmfsfilesystem is not shipped & supported by RedHat.
