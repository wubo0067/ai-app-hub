## Environment

- Red Hat Enterprise Linux 8
- Virtual Platform


## Issue

- Hard lockup on virtual system cause by resources over commitment in hypervisor level.


## Resolution

- This issue is due to a slow or over-committed hypervisor. Please reach out to your hypervisor support team or vendor for resolution.
- For further information on hypervisor overcommitment, please seeVirtualization lags and hypervisor overcommitment.


## Root Cause

- The system was stalled, very likely due to vCPU starvation from the hypervisor.
- The virtual machine didn't get enough resources in time which was causing of resource starvation and causing of crash as the operation didn't executed fast enough due to this.


## Diagnostic Steps

- The system was crashed due to hard lockup because the process was holding a CPU longer than 10 seconds.
- Each CPUs were having many pending tasks in CPUs.
