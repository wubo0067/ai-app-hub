## Resolution

Update the kernel package to version2.6.18-238.el5or later.


## Root Cause

Hard lockup occurs at CPU#1 when getting lock of cfq_exit_lock, the lockup iwas detected by NMI watchdog which crashed the system.  A patch was introduced in the kernel-2.6.18-238 viaBug 582435which removes the cfq_exit_lock.
