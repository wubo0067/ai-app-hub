- netpoll_poll_devcallsigb_poll()withnapi->poll_ownerlocking. However,net_rx_action()called byigb_polltries to lock thenapi->poll_owneritself. This deadlock issue was probably caused because the 3rd partyigbmodule did unexpected behavior or invocation.
