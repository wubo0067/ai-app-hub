## Resolution


### Red Hat Enterprise Linux 9

- The issue is under investigation at private JIRA RHEL-38348.


### Red Hat Enterprise Linux 8.10.z

- The issue has been resolved withkernel-4.18.0-553.22.1.el8_10via ErrataRHSA-2024:7000.
- The issue was tracked at private JIRA RHEL-33695.

`kernel-4.18.0-553.22.1.el8_10`
### Red Hat Enterprise Linux 8.9.z

- The issue will not be fixed for 8.9.z as there is no EUS planned.


### Red Hat Enterprise Linux 8.8.z

- The issue has been resolved withkernel-4.18.0-477.64.1.el8_8via ErrataRHSA-2024:4740.
- The issue was tracked at private JIRA RHEL-40466.

`kernel-4.18.0-477.64.1.el8_8`
### Workaround

- Settingcgroup_disable=blkiocan avoid the issue.

`cgroup_disable=blkio`
## Root Cause

- The regression started with 3b8cc6298724 ("blk-cgroup: Optimize blkcg_rstat_flush()") and the issue have been resolved by two upstream commits below:


```
d0aac2363549 ("blk-cgroup: fix list corruption from reorder of WRITE ->lqueued")
6da668063279 ("blk-cgroup: fix list corruption from resetting io stat")
```
