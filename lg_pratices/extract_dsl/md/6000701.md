## Resolution

- Please contact Veritas support team to check for some known vxio bug that could cause spinlock deadlock or contention in volget_rwspinlock(), and also install the vxio bug fix patch to avoid this sort of issue.


## Root Cause

- msdata is the data shared by per-CPU migration threads to perform stop CPU job in a cooperative way. If all threads call multi_cpu_stop() --> ack_state(),thread_ack becomes zero and then we set_state() to increment the state. MULTI_STOP_EXIT state will be reached after having this cycle 3 more times after MULTI_STOP_PREPARE. If a migration thread on some particular CPU is not given a chance to run, we are iterating over the loop in multi_cpu_stop() waiting until atomic_dec_and_test() on the thread_ack becomes true (i.e. the thread_ack becomes zero after being decremented by 1).
