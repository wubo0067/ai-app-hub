## Environment

- Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) 6.5
- LACP bonding + vlan over the bond interface
- Inteligbbased NICs

`igb`
## Issue

- We've been doing some testing here withnetemand packet corruption and I believe we may have stumbled on an igb or bonding bug that leads to a nasty system lockup on the latest RHEL release.
- We have a reproducible test case, but I'm looking for pointers on what I can collect to help deconstruct and hopefully solve the issue.

`netem`
## Resolution

- This hard lockup issue has been addressed and fixed inkernel-2.6.32-504.el6kernel version.
