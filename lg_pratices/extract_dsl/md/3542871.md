## Resolution

- The settings on the hypervisor has to be checked to avoid the situation of a loaded hypervisor. And the power savings settings on the hypervisor had to be disabled to reduce the latency between the guest and hypervisor.


## Root Cause

The settings on the hypervisor where this VM was running had to be checked. The guest was running on a loaded hypervisor contending for more vcpu resulting in a hardlockup. The latency (percent of time the VM was unable to run due to contention for access to physical CPUs) was found to be around 15%. After investigating, they discovered power saving feature(s) in the BIOS were enabled for this particular physical host- hypervisor. After disabling the power saving setting, the latency dropped substantially and the issue has been mitigated.
