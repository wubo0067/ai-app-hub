## Resolution

- Update kernel to 2.6.32-358.el6.x86_64 or later.
- Use nmi_watchdog=0 option.


## Root Cause

- Unknown. However, the aboves are valid workaround and a customer verified it.


## Diagnostic Steps

- Get a vmcore file in the panic and investigate it.
- BZ#728625(private) is tracking this problem.
