## Resolution

- A resolution would be to ask your hardware vendor to carry out the faulty CPU replacement.
- One of our customers found that the CPU, on which the hard LOCKUP happened, was actually faulty.
- And the server looked fine after replacing the faulty CPU.


## Root Cause

The root cause was that the faulty CPU did not process timer interrupts properly; this resulted in the hard LOCKUP.
